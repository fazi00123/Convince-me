Full-cycle Brand Identity Development
Process by Convince Me

At Convince Me, we follow a structured creative process that ensures originality,
strategic clarity, and impactful visual delivery.
We handle every stage from ideation to execution to build a brand that lasts.

01

Discovery & Research

Our branding experts start by understanding your business, target audience, and market landscape to define your brand’s positioning and personality.

02

Strategy Development

We create a brand strategy document outlining your mission, vision, tone of voice, and value propositions for a consistent brand experience.

03

Concept Design

This phase transforms ideas into visuals — sketches, mood boards, and creative directions that set the design foundation.

04

Logo & Visual Identity

Our designers craft multiple logo concepts, choose color palettes, and define typography, ensuring every visual aligns with your strategic goals.

05

Brand Collateral

We apply your new identity to real-world materials business cards, stationery, packaging, and digital assets — ensuring visual coherence everywhere.

06

Presentation & Feedback

You’ll receive polished brand mockups and visuals to review, refine, and finalize, ensuring complete satisfaction with your brand’s identity.

07

Brand Guidelines Delivery

We deliver a detailed brand book with usage rules, colors, fonts, logo variations, and dos & don’ts — ensuring your team and partners use your brand consistently.
