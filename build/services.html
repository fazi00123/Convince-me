Full-cycle web development
services by Convince me

At Convince me, we are focused on a stage-by-stage approach that guarantees
seamless collaboration, a sturdy software foundation, and high-quality end
product results. We tackle all stages of the website development process.

01

Requirements analysis

Our discovery phase specialists, including business analysts and designers, kick-off project collaboration by gathering and analyzing requirements, composing a detailed project roadmap, and planning every following step of the workflow.

02

Software architecture

Based on the requirements and roadmap milestones, our web architects create a basic software architecture outline to be further developed and expanded.

03

Design

The team of web designers takes up the architecture and starts filling it with visual interface assets, front-end components, and user experience elements for a high level of usability and convenient user interaction.

04

Development

06

Deployment

We help properly deploy the finished web software in the required operational environment, introducing it to the public.

07

Maintenance

Our DevOps engineers, support specialists, and managers also help maintain the smooth performance of the product in any type of operating conditions, under any constraints.
